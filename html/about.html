How Scooby and the Gang have Evolved

Scooby-Doo first premiered in 1969, and, unlike the other Hannah Barbera shows, is still alive. There are too many movies to list, and even though I think there are a couple that should be listed, I will leave them out and focus solely on the multilple series. Lets dive into the life of Scooby-Doo and how it has changed over the years.
